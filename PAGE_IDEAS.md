## Internal linking clusters

Each page links to 2–3 siblings in the same category plus 1 "cross-cluster" link (tool→article or article→tool in the same topic). Examples wired into `lib/pages.ts`:

- **Tip calculator** ↔ Budget calculator ↔ "How to make a monthly budget"
- **Pomodoro timer** ↔ To-do list ↔ "How to focus better" ↔ "How to use the pomodoro technique"
- **BMI calculator** ↔ Calorie calculator ↔ "Beginner workout plan at home"
- **JSON formatter** ↔ Base64 encoder ↔ "How to learn coding fast"
- **Word counter** ↔ Readability score checker ↔ "How to write better"

## Publishing cadence

5 pages/day = ~30 days to full catalog. Homepage + sitemap + robots ship on day 1 with the first 5 pages, so Google can crawl the site as it grows.
